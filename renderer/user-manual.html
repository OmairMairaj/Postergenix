User Manual for PosterGenix

Overview

PosterGenix is designed to automate poster creation for bulk products, enabling vendors to market each product individually with ease.

System Requirements

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or above.
  • Excel version 2010 or above for spreadsheet handling.
  • At least 4GB of RAM and 500MB of free storage for optimal performance.

Installation Instructions

  1. Download the PosterGenix application from the provided link.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions to install the application on your device.

Getting Started

  • Launching the App: Open PosterGenix from your desktop or start menu.
  • Initial Setup: Configure your app settings upon first launch.

Generating Posters

  1. Preparing Your Data:

    • Ensure your Excel sheet has the columns ITEM_NO, PACKING, and SALE_PRICE in the given order.
    • Image files should be named starting with the ITEM_NO corresponding to the Excel sheet entries.

  2. Uploading Your Excel Sheet:

    • Click on the 'Upload Excel' button and select your file.
    • The app will validate and load your data.

  3. Uploading Images:

    • Click on 'Select Image Directory' and choose the folder containing your product images.

Creating Posters

  • Individual Poster Generation: Select 'Generate Poster' next to the desired product in the list.
  • Bulk Poster Generation: Click on 'Generate All Posters' to create posters for all products.

Saving Generated Posters

Once you generate posters, either individually or in bulk, the app will automatically save them to a designated "Posters" folder within your desktop directory. To help keep your files organized, the app creates a new folder for each day's posters labeled with the current date. Each poster file is named with the prefix "poster-" followed by a timestamp to ensure uniqueness.

For easy access:

  1. Navigate to your desktop.
  2. Open the "Posters" folder.
  3. If you generated posters today, open the folder labeled with today's date.

Inside, you'll find all posters created on that date, ready for your marketing and promotional use.

Troubleshooting

  • Excel Upload Errors: Ensure no additional spaces or columns are present in your Excel sheet.
  • Image Upload Errors: Ensure that the image directory contains only image files.

FAQs

  • Can I use different Excel templates?: Currently, the app requires a specific format as described in 'Preparing Your Data'.
  • What image formats are supported?: JPG, JPEG, and PNG.
